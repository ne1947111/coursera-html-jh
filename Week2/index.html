Popular FPS Games

Battlefield V

Battlefield V is a first-person shooter video game developed by EA DICE and published by Electronic Arts. Battlefield V is the sixteenth installment in the Battlefield series. Battlefield V is focused extensively on party-based features and mechanics, scarcity of resources, and removing "abstractions" from game mechanics to increase realism.

COD: MW

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare is a 2019 first-person shooter video game developed by Infinity Ward and published by Activision. The game takes place in a realistic and modern setting. The campaign follows a CIA officer and British SAS forces as they team up with rebels from the fictional country of Urzikstan, combating together against Russian forces who have invaded the country.

Far Cry 5

Far Cry 5 is a 2018 first-person shooter game developed by Ubisoft Montreal and Ubisoft Toronto and published by Ubisoft. The game takes place in Hope County, a fictional region of Montana, United States. The main story revolves around a doomsday cult that has taken over the county at the command of its powerful leader, Joseph Seed. Players control a trapped unnamed junior deputy sheriff to liberate the county from the despotic rule of the Seeds and Eden's Gate.
